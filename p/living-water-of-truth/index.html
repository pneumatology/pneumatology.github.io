Through faith we can become spiritually true, acceptable to God, but that this depends on grace through Jesus.

Though we are now truthful with dependency on grace, and we maintain it with the spirit of Jesus inside of us!

Truth and Love are two sides of the same coin. It is an act of love to God to abide in His divine law; To love one another, and to love God (truth).
